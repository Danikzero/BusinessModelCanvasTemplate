The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

Phone service providers - send notifications
Neighborhood watch - help us understand what is needed

Key Activities

Keep open dialogue with neighborhood watch groups to meet their needs.
Maintain/update website and web communications.

Value Proposition

Provide convenience for our customers by allowing them to watch their neighborhood with greater ease and speed.

Customer Relationship

Automated services / self service. We're providing automated services that customers can use at their own discretion.

Customer Segments

Target customers are people living in the salt lake area. We are creating value particularly for those in neighborhood watch programs for their local neighborhood.

Key Resources

Email and web services, to provide communication for customers.

Channels

Advertising is limited to word of mouth. All other channels are via a website.

Cost Structure

We are value driven, and have few costs. Providing notifications via our key partnership with phone service providers appears to be our potentially largest cost. Otherwise, costs of website maintenance may be a thing.

Revenue Streams

The initial price of the application is our only source of revenue. The Application is a matter of additional convenience and ease for a somewhat niche group. I think a price between $2 - $5 is realistic.
